ABOUT US

Learn more about the Guerin Catholic Golden Gears

Welcome to the home of the Guerin Catholic Golden Gears, a passionate and innovative first-year robotics team dedicated to exploring the frontiers of technology and engineering. Founded this year, our team is composed of enthusiastic students who share a common goal: to learn, create, and compete in the exciting world of robotics.

Who We Are

We are a diverse group of students from Guerin Catholic, united by our love for robotics and our curiosity to discover how things work in the world. Our team includes maturing engineers, programmers, designers, and strategists, all eager to contribute their unique skills and perspectives to our mission.

Our Mission

Our mission at Guerin Catholic is to inspire and empower the next generation of innovators by providing hands-on experience designing, building, and programming robots. We aim to foster a collaborative learning environment where creativity and critical thinking are encouraged and every Golden Gear member can develop their skills and confidence.

What We Do

As a first-year team in the FIRST Robotics Competition, we dive into designing, building, and programming our robot from scratch. Through rigorous training and hands-on workshops, we prepare for exciting competitions where we tackle challenges and collaborate with other teams. We aim to learn, grow, and have fun while embracing the values of gracious professionalism and cooperation.

Why Join Us

Joining the Golden Gears Robotics team means becoming part of a supportive and dynamic community. Whether you're an experienced coder or new to the world of robotics, there's a place for you on our team. We offer mentorship, training sessions, and plenty of opportunities for personal and professional growth. Together, we celebrate our successes, learn from our failures, and continuously strive to improve.

COMING SOON: GUERIN CATHOLIC JOINS FIRST ROBOTICS

At Guerin Catholic High School, we are thrilled to be a part of FIRST Robotics for the 2025 season, the international high school robotics competition that combines the excitement of sport with the rigors of science and technology. With a motto of "For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology" (FIRST), this program offers an unparalleled experience for students to become science and technology leaders.

FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is a unique varsity Sport for the Mind™ designed to help young people discover how interesting and rewarding the life of engineers and researchers can be. It challenges teams of high school students and their mentors to design and build a robot in a six-week timeframe using a standard "kit of parts" and a common set of rules. Teams then compete in a series of games that test the robot's capabilities, the team's strategy, and their ability to work together under pressure.
